      Our Events

      UNIFY

      SICSR organised a cultural event, “Unify” with a lot of enthusiasm and liveliness. It is the most awaited cultural fest of all the students at SICSR. Unify is the time when students experience some fun and frolic activities apart from the regular academic schedules and enjoy oneself to the fullest. The motto of the event is “Where We Unite” which means despite all our differences we stand united and thus we are best when we are together. The event was held from 20-22 February, 2020

      GNUNIFY

      GNUNIFY is the Annual technical fest of SICSR, also powered by PLUG [ Pune Linux Users Group]. It has several workshops, tech talks completely on open source and serves as a forum to unite open minds. The tracks include Big Data, IoT, Hacking, Android, Blender, Security and System Administration, Development with Python, Mozilla, and Drupal.

      TECHNICAL EVENTS

      The first competition (Project Euler) started on March 30th 2020 and ended on 19th May 2020. In this, students have participated and developed the solution for various problem statements randomly delivered by Project Euler for 50 days. The submission repository was GitHub. The event witnessed 114 commits, making SICSR ACM chapter one of the most active repositories on GitHub. Himani Dalal received a Certificate of Appreciation.

      HACKATHON

      4th ACM-W National Hackathon by ACM India was held at SICSR on 16th-17th Nov’2019. The event was sponsored by ORACLE academy and Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).
